The definition of water pollution

Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies, usually as a result of human activities. Water bodies include lakes, rivers, oceans, aquifers and groundwater. Water pollution results when contaminants are introduced into the natural environment.

Our mission

To make people aware of the dangers of water pollution by giving them information on how to help limit water pollution.
